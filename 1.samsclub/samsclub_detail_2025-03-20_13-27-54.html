    About this item

    Powerful Cleaning and Disinfecting

    Clorox Disinfecting Wipes are triple-layered to clean, disinfect, deodorize, and remove allergens effectively. They offer 5x Cleaning Power, leaving a pleasant scent after use. These wipes clean and disinfect with antibacterial power that kills 99.9% of viruses and bacteria that can live on surfaces for up to 48 hours, including staph, E. coli, MRSA, salmonella, strep, and Kleb. Notably, Clorox Disinfecting Wipes can kill Covid-19 (SARS-CoV-2) in just 30 seconds.

    All-Purpose and Multi-Surface Use

    These all-purpose disposable wipes are perfect for removing common allergens, germs, and messes on various surfaces like kitchen counters, bathroom surfaces, and more. They are safe to use on finished wood, sealed granite, and stainless steel. Moreover, these Disinfecting Wipes are also great for non-food-contact surfaces in the home, office, classroom, pet area, dorm, and locker room. Clean with these bleach-free wipes to keep dirt and germs at bay.

    Convenient and Safe

    This pack contains five 85-count canisters that easily dispense disposable antibacterial wipes. They come in three fresh, clean scents, adding to the overall cleaning experience. Dispose of these wipes according to the manufacturer's instructions for a hassle-free clean-up. Importantly, these Clorox Disinfecting Wipes are bleach-free, offering a safe, all-in-one cleaning alternative that’s safe to use on toys and more. Experience the convenience and power of Clorox Disinfecting Wipes today.
