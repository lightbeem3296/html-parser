    About this item

    Elevate your outdoor dining experience with the Amari 7-Piece High Dining Set with Fire, a timeless masterpiece crafted to seamlessly blend comfort and style into your outdoor sanctuary.

    Timeless Elegance

    Available in your choice of Blue or Beige Sunbrella upholstery, this set effortlessly complements a variety of outdoor patio designs. Its timeless design invites you to dine in style and comfort while enjoying the great outdoors. The neutral color palette ensures it harmonizes with various outdoor aesthetics.

    Built to Endure

    This set's durability is unmatched, thanks to its robust powder-coated aluminum frame and hand-woven, all-weather resin wicker. It can withstand the rigors of outdoor use, including exposure to the elements, while maintaining its sleek appearance for years to come. The 60"" dining table with a fire feature is both functional and visually appealing.

    Sunbrella Upholstery

    The Sunbrella upholstery adds a touch of luxury and practicality. It's easy to clean and resists fading over time, ensuring your outdoor haven remains stunning even with frequent use.

    Versatile Arrangement

    The set includes a 60” dining table with a mesmerizing fire feature, six counter stools, and promises an elegant and stylish outdoor dining experience like no other. Whether you're hosting a lively dinner party or enjoying a quiet evening under the stars, the Amari set ensures enduring quality and exceptional comfort for your outdoor gatherings.
