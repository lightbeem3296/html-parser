    About this item

    To stay healthy and feeling good, you have to drink plenty of water. Aquafina Purified Drinking Water comes in a case of 32 bottles of 16.9 fl. oz. each, making it easy to meet the recommended daily water intake. Simply grab a bottle while at home or on the go. Purified using a 7-step process, Aquafina provides a fresh and clean taste.

    Water Hydrates Your Body

    Aquafina is a quality purified drinking water. Health professionals encourage people to drink at least 64 fl. oz. of water a day in order to stay hydrated. You'll need to drink more if you exercise or participate in sports activities.

    Why Choose Aquafina Purified Drinking Water?

    A thorough and scientific purification process results in cleaner water with fewer contaminants than in most tap waters. Aquafina Bottled Water is a quality drinking water for the entire family. Pure and refreshing, it offers a really clean and light taste.

    32-Count Case

    Store a case of this Aquafina Purified Drinking Water in your office. Each case comes with 32 bottles. It's also an ideal option for sports teams, concession stands and neighborhood stores.

    These 16.9 fluid ounce bottles of purified mineral water are convenient for on the go use. They'll fit easily in a gym bag for hydration before, during and after a workout. Place a bottle in your kid's backpack or lunchbox. Keep them on hand in your pantry for a quick and satisfying drink any time of the day or night.

    Why Aquafina?

    Aquafina is a proven brand that specializes in both unflavored and flavored waters. It's part of the PepsiCo family, which manufactures a wide range of food and beverage products worldwide.
