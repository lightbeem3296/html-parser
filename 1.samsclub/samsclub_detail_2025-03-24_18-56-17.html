    About this item

    Unleash Long Lasting Power

    These batteries stand out due to their high energy density, meaning they can store more energy than other types of batteries of the same size. This makes lithium batteries an excellent choice for power-hungry devices. Furthermore, lithium batteries have an impressive shelf-life, remaining effective and reliable even after long periods of storage. They also function effectively in a wide range of temperatures, making them versatile for use in different environments. Therefore, by using lithium batteries, you can unleash the power of your devices to their full potential, ensuring they function optimally and efficiently for longer periods.

    Unmatched Longevity for Your Devices

    Designed to deliver superior performance and longevity compared to standard alkaline batteries. They are engineered using advanced lithium technology, which allows them to last up to 20% longer than their alkaline counterparts. This makes them an excellent choice for high-tech and smart devices that require continuous power. In addition to their longevity, Energizer lithium batteries also feature a leak-resistant design and can perform in extreme temperatures, ranging from -40F to 140F. This makes them highly reliable and versatile for all types of use.

    Extended Shelf Life for Preparedness

    Energizer's lithium batteries are characterized by an extraordinary shelf life of up to 25 years. This is a significant advantage, especially when considering emergency or infrequently used devices. This impressive shelf life is due to the low self-discharge rate of lithium batteries. Unlike other types of batteries, lithium batteries retain their charge for a significantly longer period, even when not in use. This makes them a reliable choice for devices that require long term, steady power. Even if you store these batteries for a couple of years, you can expect them to deliver high performance when needed. This remarkable longevity, combined with their robust leak-resistant construction, high energy density, and the ability to operate in a wide range of temperatures, makes Energizer's lithium batteries a reliable and efficient power solution for a variety of devices.

    Protecting Your Devices from Leaks

    Engineered with a leak-resistant design, ensuring the longevity and safety of both the battery and the device it powers. This is a crucial feature as battery leakage can cause damage to devices, rendering them unusable. The advanced technology used in these batteries minimizes the risk of leakage, even over extended periods of use or during storage. This leak-proof design is achieved through the use of high-quality materials and precision in manufacturing, resulting in a robust and reliable battery structure. The integrity of the battery is maintained, thus protecting the device from any corrosive damage that could be caused by battery leakage. This, along with their superior performance, extended shelf life, and ability to operate in a wide range of temperatures, makes Energizer's lithium batteries a trustworthy and durable power source for a variety of devices.

    Powering Toys and Electronics with Confidence

    Whether it's a child's toy, a home entertainment system remote, or any other electronic device, Energizer lithium batteries provide reliable power, exceptional longevity, and peace of mind for all your power needs.

    Energizers Commitment to Sustainability

    Energizer demonstrates a steadfast commitment to sustainability, believing that good stewardship aligns with good business. Their dedication to minimizing environmental impact is evident through their responsible manufacturing processes, which aim to reduce waste, decrease energy consumption, and lower greenhouse gas emissions. In their product design, Energizer strives for increased energy efficiency and recyclability, showing a keen consideration for the environment.
