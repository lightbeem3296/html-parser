                Product Label

                Limit 5 per member.

                The Camilla 3-piece Sectional provides family-friendly seating for 4 people. Camilla is covered in a soft 100% Top Grain leather with split leather on the sides and backs for easy care and cleaning. Camilla features padded sides and backs for added durability. The foam seat cushions are soft and provide ample comfort and support. Camilla features a solid wood base in a honey brown finish with solid metal legs. The solid wood frame is corner blocked with reinforced joinery for lasting durability, while sinuous spring suspension supports the cushions. The cushions are removable for easy cleaning. Camilla features a large cocktail ottoman offering additional seating and comfort.

                WARNING: This product can expose you to wood dust, which is known to the State of California to cause cancer and birth defects or other reproductive harm. For more information go to: www.P65Warnings.ca.gov.


                Includes: 1 Left Arm Facing Loveseat, 1 Right Arm Facing Chaise, 1 Cocktail Ottoman
